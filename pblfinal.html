Interactive Environmental Education & Awareness Platform

Bridging India's Climate Awareness–Action Gap to Support SDG 13 (Climate Action)

Problem Statement

Despite strong policy frameworks like NAPCC and renewable energy growth, citizens lack awareness of how daily actions—energy use, waste, transport— contribute to climate change. This knowledge–action gap slows India's SDG 13 progress.

Literature Review

India has surpassed non-fossil targets and expanded forest cover, yet behavioral change mechanisms remain weak.

Research Gap

Lack of interactive, multilingual, low-bandwidth climate education platforms with gamification and community engagement.

Platform Methodology

Learning Modules

Climate change, waste, water, energy, SDGs

Gamification

Points, badges, leaderboards, eco challenges

AI Advisor

Personalized sustainability tips & tracking

Expected Impact

Awareness Growth +40%

Improved climate literacy, behavioral change, and community participation.
